The #codesurfers IRC channel

#codesurfers is an IRC channel in the OFTC network.

We mostly talk about programming, free software and their related issues, so if you like talking about such things, we welcome you to #codesurfers. Even if you don't, feel free to join and say "Hi" as our interests aren't limited to only programming.

Join us!

First of all you'll need to use an irc client. Some commonly used IRC clients on Linux are are irssi, weechat and XChat.

irssi can be used in conjunction with a GNU Screen session to keep it running indefinitely on a VPS.

We are usually the most chatty between 2300-0300. A response time less than an hour is considered okay on IRC.
